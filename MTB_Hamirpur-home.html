As the challenge pedals into its 10th year of survival through the toughest terrains, MTB Hamirpur volunteers to ride further, higher up as MTB Himalaya. The stunning scenery of Shimla, Rohru, Narkanda, Chanshal Valley along with the strenuous cycling expedition make MTB Himalaya an enduring experience.
